Field Manual

Each turn you collect Influence, draw a dossier, and may make one control attempt. Drag a dossier onto the highlighted field; an Influence token is spent and two dice are rolled.

Click or tap a card to inspect it. Click or tap again to return to the table; all other controls are locked while inspecting.

Lower-value groups are easier to control. Control 7 groups before the Cabal. Click Finish Turn when ready. Press R to reset.
